About

Select is a clubnight, mix series & collective based in Sligo, Ireland. Select aims to create an everlasting legacy of electronic music in the NortwWest.

Select aims to highlight and showcase the abundance of talent that exists in Ireland's dance music scene. This is comprised of producers, DJs, createives and artists, designers and anyone looking to express themselves via social connection and dancing.

The group is composed of four friends; Jack, Rachel, Ian, and Darragh. Select was formed in 2018 to help elevate the standards of clubbing in Sligo.
