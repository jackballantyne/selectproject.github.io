Clubbing in the North West with DJ notjack

Sligo has always been a hub for music. In recent years the evolution of dance music has mirrored that of Dublin in the 80s and 90s. The town has a rpofound love for social interaction and very often, it is happening in pubs, clubs or halls - full of music.

Faint Music and others before us have been pivital in creating an environment that is safe and welcoming for all.

Though we may not on the same frequency as those in big busy cities, we still need to releive the stress - and what better way to do it.

 

Clubbing in Sligo continues to grow and hopefully with thanks to the work of Give Us The Night and the work of Robbie Kitt and Sunil Sharpe, it can continue to bounce back after the pandemic.

Never the less, there are exciting times ahead for all.
